Account
How do I create an account?
Click the "Get Started" button on our homepage. You'll need your full name, email address, phone number, and a secure password. Account setup takes under 2 minutes.
What should I do if I forget my password?
Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. A secure reset link will be sent to your registered email address immediately.
Can I change my email address?
Email changes require verification for security reasons. Visit your Profile page and follow the email update flow, or contact our support team for assistance.
Trading
How long do transactions take?
Buy orders are typically processed within 5–15 minutes after payment confirmation. Sell orders are processed once we receive your crypto and confirm it on the blockchain — timing depends on network congestion.
What cryptocurrencies do you support?
We support Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), USDT, Solana (SOL), BNB, XRP, Litecoin (LTC), and more. Check our live rates page for the full list.
Security
Is my information secure?
Yes. We use 256-bit SSL encryption for all data transmission. Your personal information is stored behind secured networks and we never store full payment details on our servers.
Do you offer two-factor authentication?
Yes — 2FA is available for all accounts. We strongly recommend enabling it in your profile security settings to add an extra layer of protection.
Fees
What are the fees?
Our exchange rates are competitive and updated in real-time. For buy orders, you select a miner fee (Economy, Standard, or Priority) that affects transaction speed. All fees are clearly displayed before you confirm your order.
Are there deposit or withdrawal fees?
We don't charge additional deposit fees. Network transaction fees (miner fees) for crypto withdrawals are clearly shown during the order process.
